Guacamole

this is the best guacamole recipe.guacamole is meant to be easy to make and its super yummy

Ingredients

  • cilantro
  • 2 avocados
  • onion
  • tomatos
  • jalapeno pepper
  • salt
  • lime
  • garlic

    • steps

    1. slice 2 ripe avocados in half,remove the pit and scoop them into a mixing bowl
    2. use a spoon to mash them to a good chunky smooth level
    3. slice and dice the other Ingredients
    4. add the sliced and diced Ingredients to the mashed avocados and stir everything together
    5. add some salt
