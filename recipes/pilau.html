Kenyan Pilau

An African Dish,yummy! Though its roots unknown,it's mainly served in the coastal region of Kenya.Luckily it made its way throughout the country

Ingredients

  • rice
  • beef
  • garlic
  • pilau masala
  • bay leaves
  • salt
  • ground cumin
  • ground cardamon
  • ground cloves
  • peper flakes

Steps

  1. peel the onions and garlic cloves
  2. wash the beef and add it to a pan.slice in 1 onion and 2 garlic cloves with the ginger.Add bay leaves with a cup of water and some salt to taste
